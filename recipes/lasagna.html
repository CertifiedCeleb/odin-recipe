Description Of The Dish

The Most Amazing Lasagna Recipe is the best recipe for homemade Italian-style lasagna . The balance between layers of cheese, noodles, and homemade bolognese sauce is perfection! Brendas Lasagna has alwasy made other lasagna taste like normal bread

Lasagne originated in Italy during the Middle Ages and have traditionally been ascribed to the city of Naples. The first recorded recipe was set down in the early 14th-century Liber de Coquina (The Book of Cookery). It bore only a slight resemblance to the later traditional form of lasagne Recipes written in the century following the Liber de Coquina recommended boiling the pasta in chicken broth and dressing it with cheese and chicken fat.

List Of Ingredients Needed To Make Brendas Lasagna

  • 1 (16 ounce) package lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • Salt and Pepper for taste
  • 1 (16 ounce) jar spaghetti sauce
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • ½ pound shredded mozzarella cheese
  • ½ pound shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 1 pint ricotta cheese

What are the steps Needed To Make Brendas Lasagna

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
  2. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
  3. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  4. In a large skillet over medium-high heat, Add brown beef and season with salt and pepper.
  5. Stir in spaghetti sauce and garlic and simmer for 5 minutes.
  6. In a medium bowl, combine mozzarella, Cheddar and ricotta; stir well.
  7. In 9x13 inch pan, alternate layers of noodles, meat mixture and cheese mixture until pan is filled.
  8. Bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes, or until cheese is melted and bubbly.
